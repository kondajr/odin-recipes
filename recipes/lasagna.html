Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty meal is perfect for family dinners and gatherings. It's a delicious combination of flavors that will satisfy everyone!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare meat sauce: Heat olive oil in a pan. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, cook until soft. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Cook lasagna sheets: Boil lasagna sheets in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Assemble lasagna: In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, lasagna sheets, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  4. Bake: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake lasagna for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  5. Garnish and serve: Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese.
