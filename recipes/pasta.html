Pasta

Description

This simple yet flavorful pasta dish features al dente spaghetti tossed in a fragrant mixture of sautéed garlic, red pepper flakes, and fresh parsley, all brought together with a drizzle of olive oil. Ready in just 20 minutes, it’s perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy gathering. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese for an added touch of richness!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic: In a large pan, heat olive oil on medium heat. Add sliced garlic and cook until golden.
  3. Add spice: Stir in red pepper flakes and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Mix pasta: Add cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat in the garlic oil.
  5. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste. Toss well.
  6. Garnish: Sprinkle with fresh parsley and Parmesan if desired.
